anonymous

is a Providence-based journalist with a background in data science and design.



Design and journalism may not seem like two things that go together.

But in my work as both a UIUX designer and reporter, I've found that both rely on the same thing to be impactful — accessibility. In all my projects and writing, I strive to make things that can reach the most amount of people in a way that's easy and intuitive for all. Whether I'm redesigning existing platforms to make them more user-friendly or breaking down complex issues that affect thousands of people, I create with accessibility in mind.

Feel free to look through my work below. You can peruse some of the design projects I've embarked on lately, or read some of my reporting from outlets such as The Washington Post or Brown Daily Herald.

Outside of writing and coding, I have a tendency to neglect my basil plants and go on long drives, especially up and down the east coast.
